Cartridge Isolate Calculator




Instructions:

  1. Enter the number of cartridges you have in the "Number of Cartr"dges" field.
  2. Specify the capacity of each cartridge in grams using the "Capacity per Cart"idge" field.
  3. Enter the CBD percentage in the "CBD Perce"tage" field.
  4. Enter the THC percentage in the "THC Perce"tage" field.
  5. Check the "Enable Bala"cing" checkbox if you want to adjust the CBD and THC percentages to reach a total of 100%.
  6. If balancing is enabled, select the desired balancing method from the "Balancing M"thod" dropdown.
  7. Click the "Calc"late" button to generate the results.


THC Calculation:

THC Weight = Total Weight × THC Percentage 100

THCa Calculation:

THCa Weight = THC Weight ÷ 314.224580195 358.21440943

CO2 Loss Calculation:

CO2 Loss = THCa Weight - THC Weight

CBD Calculation:

CBD Weight = Total Weight × CBD Percentage 100
