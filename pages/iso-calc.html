Cartridge Isolate Calculator




  1. Enter the number of cartridges you have in the "Number of Cartridges" field.
  2. Specify the capacity of each cartridge in grams using the "Capacity per Cartridge" field.
  3. Enter the CBN percentage in the "CBN Percentage" field.
  4. Enter the THC percentage in the "THC Percentage" field.
  5. Check the "Enable Balancing" checkbox if you want to adjust the CBN and THC percentages to reach a total of 100%.
  6. If balancing is enabled, select the desired balancing method from the "Balancing Method" dropdown.
  7. Click the "Calculate" button to generate the results.

THC Calculation:

THC Weight = Total Weight × THC Percentage 100

THCa Calculation:

THCa Weight = THC Weight ÷ 314.224580195 358.21440943

CO2 Loss Calculation:

CO2 Loss = THCa Weight - THC Weight

CBN Calculation:

CBN Weight = Total Weight × CBN Percentage 100
