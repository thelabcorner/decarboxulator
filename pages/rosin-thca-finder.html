Rosin THCa and THC Calculator


To use the Rosin THCa and THC Calculator, follow these steps:

  1. Enter the initial (tared) weight of rosin in grams before decarboxylation.
  2. Enter the final (tared) weight of rosin in grams after decarboxylation [a.k.a - when the bubbles stop"].
  3. Click on the "Calculate" button to estimate THCa and THC amounts.

Results are based on stoichiometric ratios and expected CO2 loss after decarboxylation.
Use precise + calibrated weighing scales and ensure complete decarboxylation occurs in a sealed vessel. Losing any weight besides CO2 will skew the result.

After decarboxylation, allow the vessel to cool and release ("burp") any trapped CO2 to avoid skewing the results.

CAUTION - TO PREVENT EXPLOSIONS: Ensure that the expected volume of CO2 to be produced by your input mass, relative to the vessel, does not exceed its capacity!


The estimations for THCa and THC weights are derived through the following sophisticated formulas:

THCa weight = CO2 loss ÷ 44.01 × 358.48

THC weight = THCa weight × 0.877

These calculations meticulously consider the molecular weights and proportions of CO2, THCa, and THC, delivering highly precise estimations.
